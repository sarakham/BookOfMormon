Behold, I am a man, and am thy servant; therefore, whatsoever thou desirest which is right, that will I do. I am not. Wilt thou hearken unto my words, if I tell thee by what power I do these things? And this is the thing that I desire of thee. Believest thou that there is a God? Believest thou that there is a Great Spirit? This is God. Believest thou that this Great Spirit, who is God, created all things which are in heaven and in the earth? The heavens is a place where God dwells and all his holy angels. Yea, and he looketh down upon all the children of men; and he knows all the thoughts and intents of the heart; for by his hand were they all created from the beginning.
