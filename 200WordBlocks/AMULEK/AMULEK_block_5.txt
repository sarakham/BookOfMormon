And now behold, for this great evil thou shalt have thy reward. Yea, there is a true and living God. No. An angel hath made them known unto me. Yea. I say unto you he shall not, for it is impossible for him to deny his word. Behold thou hast lied, for thou sayest that I spake as though I had authority to command God because I said he shall not save his people in their sins. And I say unto you again that he cannot save them in their sins; for I cannot deny his word, and he hath said that no unclean thing can inherit the kingdom of heaven; therefore, how can ye be saved, except ye inherit the kingdom of heaven?
