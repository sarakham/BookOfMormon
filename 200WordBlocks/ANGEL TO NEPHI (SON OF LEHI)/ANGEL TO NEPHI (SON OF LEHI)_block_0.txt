 Nephi, what beholdest thou? Knowest thou the condescension of God? Behold, the virgin whom thou seest is the mother of the Son of God, after the manner of the flesh. Look! Behold the Lamb of God, yea, even the Son of the Eternal Father! Knowest thou the meaning of the tree which thy father saw? Yea, and the most joyous to the soul. Look! Look and behold the condescension of God! Look! Look! Look! Behold the world and the wisdom thereof; yea, behold the house of Israel hath gathered together to fight against the twelve apostles of the Lamb. Thus shall be the destruction of all nations, kindreds, tongues, and people, that shall fight against the twelve apostles of the Lamb. Look, and behold thy seed, and also the seed of thy brethren. Behold the twelve disciples of the Lamb, who are chosen to minister unto thy seed. Thou rememberest the twelve apostles of the Lamb? Behold they are they who shall judge the twelve tribes of Israel; wherefore, the twelve ministers of thy seed shall be judged of them; for ye are of the house of Israel.
