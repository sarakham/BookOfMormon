Verily I say unto you, I commanded my servant Samuel, the Lamanite, that he should testify unto this people, that at the day that the Father should glorify his name in me that there were many saints who should arise from the dead, and should appear unto many, and should minister unto them. Was it not so? How be it that ye have not written this thing, that many saints did arise and appear unto many and did minister unto them? Thus said the Father unto Malachi? These scriptures, which ye had not with you, the Father commanded that I should give unto you; for it was wisdom in him that they should be given unto future generations. I will try the faith of my people. What will ye that I shall give unto you? Verily, verily, I say unto you, why is it that the people should murmur and dispute because of this thing?
