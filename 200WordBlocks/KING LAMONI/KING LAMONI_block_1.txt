Yea, I will believe all thy words. I do not know what that meaneth. Yea. Yea, I believe that he created all things which are in the earth; but I do not know the heavens. Is it above the earth? I believe all these things which thou hast spoken. Art thou sent from God? O Lord, have mercy; according to thy abundant mercy which thou hast had upon the people of Nephi, have upon me, and my people. Blessed be the name of God, and blessed art thou. For as sure as thou livest, behold, I have seen my Redeemer; and he shall come forth, and be born of a woman, and he shall redeem all mankind who believe on his name.
