And it came to pass that king Lamoni caused that his servants should stand forth and testify to all the things which they had seen concerning the matter. And when they had all testified to the things which they had seen, and he had learned of the faithfulness of Ammon in preserving his flocks, and also of his great power in contending against those who sought to slay him, he was astonished exceedingly, and said: And they answered the king, and said: And now, when the king heard these words, he said unto them: Now this was the tradition of Lamoni, which he had received from his father, that there was a Great Spirit.
