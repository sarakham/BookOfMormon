And Ammon began to speak unto him with boldness, and said unto him: And he answered, and said unto him: And then Ammon said: And he said, And Ammon said: And Ammon said unto him again: And he said: And Ammon said unto him: And king Lamoni said: And Ammon said: And king Lamoni said: Ammon said unto him: Now when Ammon had said these words, he began at the creation of the world, and also the creation of Adam, and told him all the things concerning the fall of man, and rehearsed and laid before him the records and the holy scriptures of the people, which had been spoken by the prophets, even down to the time that their father, Lehi, left Jerusalem. And he also rehearsed unto them (for it was unto the king and to his servants) all the journeyings of their fathers in the wilderness, and all their sufferings with hunger and thirst, and their travail, and so forth.
