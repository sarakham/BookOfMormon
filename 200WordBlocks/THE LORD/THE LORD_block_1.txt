If ye will not harden your hearts, and ask me in faith, believing that ye shall receive, with diligence in keeping my commandments, surely these things shall be made known unto you. In thy seed shall all the kindreds of the earth be blessed. Look upon the ball, and behold the things which are written. Arise, and get thee into the mountain. Thou shalt construct a ship, after the manner which I shall show thee, that I may carry thy people across these waters. I will make thy food become sweet, that ye cook it not; And I will also be your light in the wilderness; and I will prepare the way before you, if it so be that ye shall keep my commandments; wherefore, inasmuch as ye shall keep my commandments ye shall be led towards the promised land; and ye shall know that it is by me that ye are led.
