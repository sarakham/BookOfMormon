Behold, the Lamanites have I cursed, and I will set a mark on them that they and their seed may be separated from thee and thy seed, from this time henceforth and forever, except they repent of their wickedness and turn to me that I may have mercy upon them. I will set a mark upon him that mingleth his seed with thy brethren, that they may be cursed also. I will set a mark upon him that fighteth against thee and thy seed. I say he that departeth from thee shall no more be called thy seed; and I will bless thee, and whomsoever shall be called thy seed, henceforth and forever; Repent, and I will receive you.
