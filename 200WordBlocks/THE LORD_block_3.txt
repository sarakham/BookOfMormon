Stretch forth thine hand again unto thy brethren, and they shall not wither before thee, but I will shock them, and this will I do, that they may know that I am the Lord their God. yea, and all the people who are of the house of Israel, will I gather in, from the four quarters of the earth. Behold, I have declared the former things from the beginning; and they went forth out of my mouth, and I showed them. I did show them suddenly. And I did it because I knew that thou art obstinate, and thy neck is an iron sinew, and thy brow brass; And I have even from the beginning declared to thee; before it came to pass I showed them thee; and I showed them for fear lest thou shouldst say mine idol hath done them, and my graven image, and my molten image hath commanded them. Thou hast seen and heard all this; and will ye not declare them? And that I have showed thee new things from this time, even hidden things, and thou didst not know them.
